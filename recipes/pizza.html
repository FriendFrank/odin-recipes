Ingredients

  • 1 glass water
  • 1 working kitchen
  • 1 box of frozen pizza
  • about 15 minutes of heat
  1. Start preheating oven to 400 F
  2. While preheating, open frozen pizza box, careful to remove pizza pie
  3. Place pizza pie on pizza pan - not required
  4. Once oven is ready, place pizza pie into oven, and set timer for 15 minutes
  5. Enjoy glass of water while pizza cooks
  6. After 15 minutes is up, remove pizza from oven, cool and enjoy
