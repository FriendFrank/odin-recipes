Ingredients

  • 1 glass water
  • 1 working kitchen
  • 1 bag of frozen fish sticks
  • about 5 minutes of heat
  1. Remove bag of frozen fish sticks from freezer
  2. Without hurting yourself, open the bag
  3. Place desired amount of fish sticks onto a microwave safe plate
  4. Place plate of fish sticks into microwave, and cook on high power for at least 5 minutes
  5. Enjoy glass of water while fish sticks cook
  6. After 5 minutes is up, remove fish sticks from microwave, cool and enjoy
